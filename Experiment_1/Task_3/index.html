Top Places to Visit in Dehradun

Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave is a natural cave formation located in the outskirts of Dehradun City. The place is famous for its unique phenomenon of the disappearing stream.

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara, which means 'Thousand-fold spring', is a famous tourist destination known for its sulfur water springs.

Mindrolling Monastery

Mindrolling Monastery is one of the largest Buddhist centers in India, known for its architectural beauty and tranquil environment.

Forest Research Institute

The Forest Research Institute is an educational and tourist destination, known for its Greco-Roman architecture and beautiful surroundings.
